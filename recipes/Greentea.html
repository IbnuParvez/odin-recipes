Description

Green tea, made from unoxidized Camellia sinensis leaves, is a popular beverage and dietary supplement with potential health benefits like improved cognitive function, fat burning, and antioxidant properties.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 4 bages of green tea
  • 1 bunch fresh mint
  • ice , as needed

Steps

  1. For green tea punch, bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Remove from heat and add tea bags. Steep, covered, 5 minutes. Remove tea bags, or strain to remove loose tea. Let tea cool completely, about 1 hour.
  2. Muddle mint in a large pitcher. Add cooled tea, vodka, honey-rhubarb syrup, and lime juice. Chill at least 30 minutes, or up to 12 hours.
